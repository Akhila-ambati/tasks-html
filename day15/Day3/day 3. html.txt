<h1>India</h1>
<h3>using target="_self":</h3>
<p><a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Human"target="_self">Modern humans</a> 
arrived on the <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indian_subcontinent"target="_self">Indian subcontinent</a> from Africa no later than 55,000 years ago.Their long occupation, initially in varying forms of isolation as hunter-gatherers, has made the region highly diverse, second only to Africa in human genetic diversity.<br>
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Neolithic"target="_self">Settled life</a> emerged on the subcontinent in the western margins of the 
<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indus_River"target="_self">Indus river</a> basin 9,000 years ago, evolving gradually into the<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Indus_Valley_Civilisation"target="_self">Indus valley civilisation</a>  of the third millennium BCE.<br><hr>
<h3>using target="_blank":</h3>
In the early medieval era,<a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Christianity"target="_blank">Christianity</a>, Islam, <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Judaism"target="_blank">Judaism</a>, and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Zoroastrianism"target="_blank">zoroastrianism</a> became established on India's southern and western coasts. Muslim armies from <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Central_Asia"target="_blank">Central Asia</a> intermittently overran India's northern plains,eventually founding the Delhi Sultanate, and drawing northern India into the cosmopolitan <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Islamic_Golden_Age"target="_blank">networks of medieval Islam</a>.<br><hr>
<h3>using target="_self",target="_blank"&title:</h3>
India has been a <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Federal_republic"title="federal wikipedia"target="_self">federal republic</a>since 1950, governed through a democratic <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Parliamentary_system"title="parliamentary wikipedia"target="_blank">parliamentary system</a>. It is a pluralistic, multilingual and <a href="https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Multiculturalism"title="multiethnic wikipedia"target="_blank">multi-ethnic society</a>.<hr>
